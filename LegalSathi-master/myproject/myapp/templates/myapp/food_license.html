FAQs on FSSAI Registration

Q1. What is the importance of the Food License Registration?

The major importance of the FSSAI License is that it ensures that your food is verified chemically and hence is safe to consume.

Q2. What is the Procedure for Food License Registration?

The procedure involves determining your eligibility, completing the application form, gathering supporting documents, submitting the application, paying the fees, and undergoing inspection before the issuance of the certificate.

Q3. What are the types of FSSAI License Registration?

There are two types: Basic Registration and State/Central Licence, depending on the size and turnover of the business.

Q4. How can I get my FSSAI license renewed?

The renewal process involves submitting a renewal application before the expiration date, along with the required documents and fees.
