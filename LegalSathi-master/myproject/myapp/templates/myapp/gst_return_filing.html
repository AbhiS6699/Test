How to File GST Returns Online

GSTR-1

The GSTR-1 is a monthly return form filed by businesses to report the details of outward supplies. It must be filed by the 10th of every month.

GSTR-3B

GSTR-3B is a self-declared return form for summarizing GST liabilities and ITC. It must be filed by the 20th of every month.

GSTR-9

GSTR-9 is an annual return form summarizing all the returns filed during the financial year. It is due by December 31st.

GSTR-4

GSTR-4 is a quarterly return form for composition scheme taxpayers, summarizing outward and inward supplies.

FAQs on GST Return Filing

Q1: What is GST return filing?

GST return filing is the process of reporting your income, expenses, and other financial details to the tax department. It includes details of sales, purchases, output GST, and input tax credit.

Q2: What are the types of GST returns?

The main types of GST returns include GSTR-1, GSTR-3B, GSTR-9, GSTR-4, and others, each with specific purposes and due dates.

Q3: Can a person without a GST number collect GST?

No, only registered businesses with a valid GSTIN can collect GST from customers.

Q4: What is the penalty for late filing of GST returns?

The late fee is Rs. 100 per day under CGST & SGST, totaling Rs. 200/day. Interest at 18% per annum is also applicable.

Q5: What is the composition scheme under GST?

The composition scheme allows small businesses to pay GST at a fixed rate of turnover and file quarterly returns.
