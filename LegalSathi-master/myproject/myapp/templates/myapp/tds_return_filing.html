About TDS Return Filing

TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) return filing is a fundamental financial procedure in India. It involves reporting the tax deductions made by payers at the source of various transactions. This reporting ensures that taxes are collected and remitted accurately to the government. TDS return filing is not only a legal obligation but also a mechanism that supports transparency, revenue generation, and effective taxation. Types of Forms

Form 24Q

This form is used to report TDS on salaries. It involves detailing the salaries paid, tax deductions made, and other relevant information. At LegalSathi, our adept team specialises in handling Form 24Q with accuracy, ensuring your TDS on salaries is managed seamlessly.

Form 26Q

This form is used to report TDS on all payments other than salaries. LegalSathi ensures that your Form 26Q filings are accurate and on time.

Form 27Q

This form is used to report TDS on payments made to non-residents, including salaries and other payments. Our team is well-versed in managing Form 27Q filings, ensuring compliance with all relevant regulations.

Form 27EQ

This form is used to report the collection of tax at source (TCS). LegalSathi will assist you in filing Form 27EQ, ensuring that all details are accurately reported.
